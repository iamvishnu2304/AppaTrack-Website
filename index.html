About Me

Our Safety Features

Alcohol Detection & Helmet Compliance

Bike only starts when helmet is worn and no alcohol is detected. Integrated sensors ensure rider safety before ignition.

Intelligent Speed Control

Automatic warnings and engine cutoff when overspeeding is detected, preventing reckless riding.

Protective Airbag System

Deploys when dangerous angles or impacts are detected, reducing injury risks.

Occupant Detection

Prevents operation with more than two riders, ensuring optimal weight distribution.

Smart Load Management

Prevents bike operation when overloaded beyond safety limits.

Live GPS Tracking

Real-time location monitoring via the AppaTrack mobile app for security and emergency response.
